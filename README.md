# Ex-4.-Scenario-Based-Report-Development-Utilizing-Diverse-Prompting-Techniques
Objective: The goal of this experiment is to design and develop an AI-powered chatbot that can handle customer inquiries, provide support, and improve customer experience in a retail environment. Create prompts using various AI prompting techniques to guide your experiment, data collection, analysis, and report creation.

## Aim: 

To design and develop an AI-powered chatbot that can handle customer inquiries, provide support services, and improve customer experience in a retail environment using different AI prompting techniques.

## Algorithm: 
Step 1: Define the Objective

Identify the purpose of the chatbot such as answering customer queries, product recommendations, order tracking, and customer support.

Step 2: Collect Retail Scenarios

Prepare different customer interaction scenarios like product inquiry, return policy, payment issue, and delivery status.

Step 3: Select Prompting Techniques

Choose prompting methods such as Zero-shot Prompting, Few-shot Prompting, Chain-of-Thought Prompting, and Instruction-based Prompting.

Step 4: Design Chatbot Prompts

Create prompts for each customer scenario using the selected prompting techniques.

Step 5: Execute and Test Prompts

Provide prompts to the AI chatbot and collect responses for different customer queries.

Step 6: Analyze Responses

Evaluate the chatbot responses based on accuracy, relevance, clarity, response speed, and customer satisfaction.

Step 7: Compare Prompting Techniques

Compare how different prompting techniques improve chatbot performance and response quality.

Step 8: Document Findings

Record observations, strengths, limitations, and improvements required in chatbot interaction.

Step 9: Generate Final Report

Prepare a report summarizing chatbot performance, prompting techniques used, analysis, and final outcomes.

## Prompt:
 Design an AI-powered retail chatbot that can answer customer inquiries, provide product recommendations, track orders, and handle return or replacement requests.
 Use different prompting techniques such as Zero-shot, Few-shot, Chain-of-Thought, and Instruction-based prompting to improve chatbot response quality.
 Analyze the chatbot responses based on accuracy, relevance, clarity, and customer satisfaction across multiple retail scenarios.
